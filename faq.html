Getting Started
Travylway is a web-based ride booking platform that connects riders with independent drivers. There is no app to download — everything runs directly from your phone's browser or any device with internet access.
Visit travylway.com and complete the driver sign-up form. Once your information is reviewed and approved, you will receive a welcome guide with everything you need to get started.
No. There is no sign-up fee or subscription cost to drive on the Travylway platform.
You will need the following before your first ride:
  • A valid, government-issued driver's license in good standing
  • Active car insurance that meets your state's minimum coverage requirements
  • A vehicle that is 2018 or newer, in clean condition, with valid registration
  • Access to a free flight tracking tool such as FlightAware or Flightradar24 for airport pickups
  • An active, verified account with at least one of the following for payouts: Venmo, Cash App, or PayPal
Rides & Policies

Earnings & Payouts
Driver payouts are sent through peer-to-peer payment platforms. You must have an active, verified account with at least one of the following: Venmo, Cash App, or PayPal.
Income will vary based on your location, availability, ride volume, and the number of platforms you choose to work with. Travylway makes no guarantee of specific earnings.

We encourage you to diversify your income across multiple services.

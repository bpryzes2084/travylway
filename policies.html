Payment Policy - Immediate Payment Required

Payment Deadline

All payments must be completed within 24 hours of ride completion, regardless of payment method selected.

Policy Details

Payment is due within 24 hours of drop-off time. This applies to all payment methods including credit/debit card, Venmo, PayPal, and Cash App. Cash payments are not accepted. No exceptions will be made, and this policy applies to all riders including airline crew members and general travelers. Business is business.

Final Notice Process

When payment is not received within the required timeframe, you will receive a final notice regarding your unpaid ride. Per TravylWay's payment policy, all payments must be completed within 24 hours of ride completion. A payment reminder will be sent, and if payment has still not been received, you will be given until 11:59 PM of the following day to complete payment. If payment is not received by this deadline, enforcement actions will be taken immediately as outlined below.

Consequences of Non-Payment After Services Rendered

If payment is not received by the final deadline, the following actions will be taken immediately: Your Venmo or Cash App username will be blocked from future transactions. Your contact information will be blocked. Your IP address will be blocked from accessing the TravylWay website. You will be permanently banned from using TravylWay services. Service restoration requires full payment of outstanding balance. Repeated payment issues will result in permanent ban from the platform.

Why This Policy Exists

TravylWay operates on a model that ensures our drivers receive their earnings promptly. Unlike traditional rideshare services, we don't hold driver payments. When passengers delay or fail to pay, drivers lose income they've already earned, it creates uncertainty in driver compensation, and it damages the trust-based relationship we've built with our driver partners. Our 75% driver compensation model only works when payments are processed immediately. This policy protects both our drivers and maintains the reliability airline crew members depend on.

Your Responsibility

By booking with TravylWay, you agree to have a valid payment method available at the time of your ride, pay immediately upon ride completion, and not schedule or delay payment requests beyond the ride completion time. Ignoring Cash App or Venmo payment requests, scheduled Venmo payment requests for future dates, or failing to respond to payment requests will be treated as non-payment and subject to the consequences outlined above. TravylWay is not a credit service. Payment is expected at time of service.

If you're experiencing a legitimate payment issue such as a card decline or app error, contact TravylWay immediately. Good-faith payment problems will be handled on a case-by-case basis, but intentional payment delays will result in immediate blocking from the platform.

Passenger Etiquette Policy

Policy Overview

TravylWay expects all passengers to conduct themselves with professionalism, respect, and consideration during all interactions with drivers and throughout the ride.

Core Principles

All passenger conduct must be built on three fundamental principles: respect for the driver and vehicle, restraint in behavior and communication, and responsibility for one's actions and belongings.

Required Behaviors

Respectful Communication. Address drivers by name only. Use please and thank you. Practice active listening when drivers provide information about the route or ride details. Communicate clearly and professionally regarding pickup location, destination, and any special requirements.

Punctuality. Be ready at the designated pickup location at the scheduled time. Respect the driver's time and schedule. If running late, communicate immediately through the established communication channel.

Professional Conduct. Maintain appropriate behavior throughout the ride. Keep conversations respectful and professional. Avoid controversial topics, gossip, or inappropriate comments. Respect the driver's personal space and professional boundaries.

Vehicle Respect. Treat the vehicle as you would want your own property treated. Do not eat or drink without driver permission. Dispose of any trash properly. Keep belongings contained and organized. Do not damage or soil the vehicle interior.

Technology Management. Keep phone conversations brief and at reasonable volume. Use headphones for music or videos. Silence notifications and ringtones. Avoid distracting the driver with device screens or sounds.

Positive Environment. Maintain a calm, respectful atmosphere. Avoid loud or disruptive behavior. Keep music or entertainment at appropriate levels if permitted. Be mindful of the driver's working environment and concentration needs.

Prohibited Behaviors

The following behaviors are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate ride termination and permanent ban from TravylWay services: verbal abuse, harassment, or threatening language toward the driver; physical contact or invasion of the driver's personal space; intoxication or impairment that creates safety concerns; smoking, vaping, or use of controlled substances in the vehicle; intentional damage to the vehicle; refusing to wear a seatbelt; interfering with the driver's operation of the vehicle; and any behavior that creates an unsafe or unprofessional environment.

Consequences for Policy Violations

Minor violations will result in a warning and documentation in the passenger's account. Repeated violations will result in suspension of TravylWay services. Serious violations including harassment, threats, property damage, or safety concerns will result in immediate permanent ban from the platform. No refunds will be issued for rides terminated due to passenger misconduct.

Driver Authority

Drivers have full authority to enforce this etiquette policy. If a driver determines that a passenger's behavior violates this policy, the driver may terminate the ride immediately. The passenger will still be responsible for payment and may face additional consequences as outlined above.

By booking with TravylWay, you agree to conduct yourself professionally and respectfully at all times. You are responsible for your behavior, your belongings, and any guests traveling with you. Business is business. Respectful conduct is non-negotiable.

Unaccompanied Minor Policy

Policy Statement

TravylWay strictly prohibits transportation of unaccompanied minors. All passengers must be 18 years of age or older to use TravylWay services. This is a zero-tolerance policy with no exceptions.

Who Can Ride

To book or ride with TravylWay, you must be at least 18 years of age. Passengers under 18 years of age are not permitted to ride, even if the ride is requested or paid for by a parent, guardian, or other adult.

Why This Policy Exists

This policy protects both drivers and passengers. Transportation of unaccompanied minors creates legal liability issues, voids insurance coverage, and places drivers in potentially compromising situations. TravylWay will not expose our drivers or our business to these risks under any circumstances.

Driver Instructions

Drivers must verify passenger age before allowing anyone into the vehicle. If a passenger appears to be under 18 years of age, the driver must request identification. If the passenger is under 18 or refuses to provide identification proving they are 18 or older, the driver must refuse the ride immediately. Drivers will not face penalties or loss of compensation for refusing rides involving passengers under 18 years of age.

Any driver who knowingly transports a passenger under 18 years of age will face immediate termination from the TravylWay platform. Any adult who attempts to book a ride for a minor will be permanently banned from using TravylWay services.

This policy applies regardless of circumstances including but not limited to: distance of travel, presence of siblings or friends, parental consent, emergency situations, or any other reason. Business is business. Safety and legal compliance are non-negotiable.

Driver No-Cancellation Policy

Policy Overview

Drivers are not allowed to cancel airport rides once accepted. This is a zero-tolerance policy with immediate consequences for violations.

Why This Policy Exists

The nature of airline operations requires absolute reliability. Flight attendants, pilots, and airline crew members must report to work on time. A cancelled ride can result in missed flights, disciplinary action against crew members, and disruption to airline operations affecting hundreds of passengers.

Policy Details

Once a driver accepts an airport ride, cancellation is not permitted under any circumstances. This applies to all airport pickups including rides for airline crew members, general travelers, and passengers with flight connections.

Any driver who cancels an accepted airport ride will face immediate termination from the TravylWay platform. There are no warnings. There are no second chances. Account will be permanently deactivated and the driver will be banned from future work with TravylWay.

Driver Responsibility

Before accepting any airport ride, drivers must ensure they can complete the assignment. This includes verifying vehicle condition, fuel level, traffic conditions, and personal availability. If there is any doubt about ability to complete the ride, do not accept it.

Business is business. Reliability is non-negotiable.

On-Time Policy

Policy Overview

TravylWay is committed to ensuring all passengers arrive at their destinations on time. This policy outlines our commitment to punctuality and the shared responsibilities between TravylWay, drivers, and passengers to maintain on-time performance.

Why Punctuality Matters

Airlines operate on strict schedules. Flight attendants, pilots, and airline crew members must report to work on time or face disciplinary action. Travelers must arrive at the airport with sufficient time for check-in, security screening, and boarding. Missing a flight due to late arrival creates significant financial and logistical consequences.

TravylWay's Commitment

TravylWay commits to providing reliable, on-time transportation service. Our drivers are instructed to arrive at pickup locations on time and to operate with sufficient time buffers to account for normal traffic conditions. We monitor flight schedules through FlightAware integration to ensure airport pickups align with actual arrival times.

Pickup Time Standards

For airport drop-offs, passengers should be picked up with sufficient time to arrive at the airport according to TSA and airline recommendations. For domestic flights, this typically means arrival at the airport two hours before departure. For international flights, this typically means arrival at the airport three hours before departure. For airline crew members reporting to work, pickup times will be coordinated to ensure arrival according to crew check-in requirements, typically 60-90 minutes before departure.

No driver is permitted to cancel on any airline crew member or traveler going to the airport within 48 hours of the scheduled pickup time. Once a ride is accepted and the pickup time is within 48 hours, the driver has made an absolute commitment that cannot be broken.

Passenger Responsibility for On-Time Service

Passengers must be ready at the designated pickup location at the scheduled pickup time. Delays caused by passengers not being ready will impact on-time arrival and may result in missed flights. Passengers must provide accurate flight information and pickup addresses. Passengers should account for building exit time, elevator delays, and walking distance to pickup point when determining their ready time.

Consequences of Lateness

If a passenger misses their flight due to late arrival caused by the passenger not being ready at pickup time, TravylWay is not responsible and no refund will be issued. Drivers who demonstrate a pattern of lateness or poor time management will be removed from the TravylWay platform. Any driver who cancels on an airline crew member or traveler within 48 hours of pickup time will face immediate termination from the TravylWay platform.

By booking with TravylWay, you acknowledge that airline travel requires punctuality and that you share responsibility for ensuring adequate time is allocated for your journey.
